BEVERAGES

RAKI
Raki is "Anise" flavored alcoholic beverage from Turkey,Raki was first produced from the residue of grapes left over from wine making. When a shortage of residue started, spirits from abroad were imported and processed with aniseed.For good quality raki, seedless raisins and aniseed in Çesme were preferred. Raki is not just a drink, it's an experience, a philosophy a lifestyle!

WINE
Wine is an alcoholic drink made from fermented grapes, and the five countries with the largest wine-producing regions are in Italy, Spain, France, the United States, and China. Yeast consumes the sugar in the grapes and converts it to ethanol, carbon dioxide, and heat. Different varieties of grapes and strains of yeasts produce different styles of wine. These variations result from the complex interactions between the biochemical development of the grape, the reactions involved in fermentation, the terroir, and the production process.
