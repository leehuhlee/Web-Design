F.A.Q.

RESERVATION

Reservations can be made via e-mail 3 days in advance. Depending on your party size and reservation moment, we might require a credit card number to hold your reservation.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Our Cancelation Policy varies depending on the party size and moment of reservation, and are clearly advised by writing prior to booking is being formally made.

LARGE GROUPS/CAPACITY

Groups of 12 to 25 people require an advanced reservation and special Group Menu. Due to limited space, groups of 25 or more are asked to rent the entire restaurant.

SEATING

We will always do our best to try and accommodate your seating requests, but we do not guarantee assigned seats.

TAKE OUT AND DELIVERY

We offer take out but we don't offer delivery.

HOLIDAYS

We have special menu for Christmas, New Year's Eve and also for Ramadan.

SERVICE CHARGE

An automatic 10% service charge is added to every bill.

METHODS OF PAYMENT

We accept Euro, US Dollar, American Express, Visa and MasterCard. Sorry, we do not accept any checks.
